  - **Vote explanations required** (`A.1.5.2.2`): must (1) demonstrate understanding, (2) articulate reasoning, (3) address substantive aspect; posted within 1 week of vote end

Claims of ignorance are not accepted as defenses; a subsequent ignorance claim is a Tier 2 breach. Only applies to yes votes вАФ no/abstain retain standing to raise concerns. Exception: materially misleading proposal text or newly-emerged material facts (burden on the AD).
